## 🤖 The AI Agents

| Agent | What It Does |
|---|---|
| 📋 **Intake Agent** | Collects client info, validates documents, opens cases |
| 📑 **Document Agent** | Extracts data from passports, visas, foreign records, translates |
| ✍️ **Forms Agent** | Auto-fills I-130, I-485, N-400, H-1B, EB-5, and other USCIS forms |
| 📅 **Deadline Agent** | Tracks every case deadline, alerts on expirations |
| 💬 **Client Comms Agent** | Proactively updates clients, answers FAQs, sends reminders |
| 🔄 **Renewal Agent** | Surfaces renewal opportunities from existing client data |
| 📈 **BD Agent** | Runs business development campaigns, qualifies leads |
| 🌐 **Ecosystem Agent** | Coordinates with translators, courier services, foreign agents |

## 🎯 The Three Pillars

### 1. AI Agents
A suite of autonomous AI workers handling case management, client communications, renewals, and business development - purpose-built for immigration workflows.

### 2. Managed Technology Operations
We deploy, configure, monitor, and optimize the entire agent ecosystem within your firm. Updates, integrations, support, and ongoing improvements - fully managed by our team.

### 3. Global Immigration Ecosystem
Curated network of pre-vetted immigration service providers worldwide - translators, document authentication services, foreign attorneys, medical exam centers, courier services, and more - all accessible through one platform.

## 👥 Two Audiences

### Immigration Law Firms
Solo, mid-size, and large law firms practicing immigration law. Pain points: manual case preparation, scaling case volume, growing revenue from existing clients, and managing technology.

**Message:** *"GlobalCodio Agents handle case prep, deadlines, client comms, and renewals - so your attorneys focus on what matters."*

### Corporate Immigration Departments
In-house mobility, HR, and legal operations teams at mid-to-large employers managing employee visa cases. Pain points: scaling case volume, compliance, vendor management, and cost predictability.

**Message:** *"GlobalCodio Agents handle visa case tracking, compliance monitoring, and vendor coordination - so your mobility team focuses on strategy."*

## 💰 Pricing Model - Base + Performance

### How It Works
A predictable monthly base fee covers the platform and managed operations. A small performance share is tied directly to measurable cost savings and revenue gains delivered. Firms only pay more when they earn more or save more.

### Value Levers Measured
1. **Reduced case preparation time** - Cost savings from Document, Forms, and Intake Agents
2. **AI-powered customer support** - Cost savings from Client Comms Agent
3. **Renewal detection** - Revenue from previously missed renewals (Renewal Agent)
4. **BD campaign automation** - Revenue from new client acquisition (BD Agent)

### Year One Promise
Firms partnering with GlobalCodio typically see a **3x return in year one** through reduced operational costs and significantly increased case volume.
